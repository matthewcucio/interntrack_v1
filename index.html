Features

Everything you need
to log your internship

Three powerful tools that do the math so you can focus on the work.

⏱

Smart Time Logging

Pick your time in and time out. Toggle lunch break deduction on or off. Hours are calculated automatically — no spreadsheet needed.

📅

Philippine Holiday Calendar

2025 and 2026 PH public and special non-working holidays are pre-loaded. Regular and special holidays are highlighted automatically.

📊

Live Progress Dashboard

A glowing progress ring shows exactly how far you've come. See rendered vs. remaining hours, days logged, leave days, and average daily hours.

How It Works

Set Up Your Profile

Enter your required OJT hours, company name, and start date. Takes 30 seconds, saves forever.

Log Your Daily Hours
